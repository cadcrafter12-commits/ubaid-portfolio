About the studio

Architectural drafting with a disciplined documentation mindset.

Ubaid Al Baraka Drafting Studio helps architects, designers, contractors, and property owners translate ideas into clear technical drawings and BIM documentation.

Studio approach

Every line should make the project easier to understand.

Our work focuses on accurate geometry, clean sheet organization, and practical coordination. Whether the source is a hand sketch, PDF markup, survey information, or an existing CAD file, the goal is consistent: drawings that communicate clearly.

Projects are prepared with attention to scale, line weights, annotations, sheet structure, and revision clarity so clients can review, estimate, present, and build with confidence.

Values

Reliable drafting support for fast-moving teams.

Accuracy

Drawings are built carefully around dimensions, alignment, scale, and coordination between views.

Clarity

Sheets are organized to be readable for clients, consultants, contractors, and approval reviewers.

Responsiveness

Communication stays direct, revision notes stay visible, and project priorities stay easy to track.

Professional standards

Layering, annotations, view naming, and sheet setup follow a tidy architectural documentation workflow.

Process

A simple path from brief to clean deliverables.

  1. 01

    Review

    Project files, sketches, dimensions, redlines, and scope are reviewed before work begins.

  2. 02

    Draft

    Plans, elevations, sections, or models are prepared with organized standards and clean layout.

  3. 03

    Coordinate

    Views, sheets, notes, and drawing relationships are checked for consistency and clarity.

  4. 04

    Deliver

    Final files are exported in the requested format, including CAD, Revit, PDF, or image outputs.
